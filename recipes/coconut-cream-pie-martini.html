Coconut Cream Pie Martini

Description

A dessert you can drink? Yes, please! It tastes like sweet coconut and is a real crowd-pleaser for those who don't like the taste of traditional alcoholic drinks

Ingredients

  • 1 fluid ounce coconut-flavored rum (such as MalibuÂ®)
  • 1 fluid ounce vanilla schnapps
  • 3 fluid ounces half-and-half cream

Steps

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Pour rum, vanilla schnapps, and cream over the ice. Cover, shake, and strain martini into a cocktail glass.
